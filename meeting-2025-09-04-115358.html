UNDERSTANDING LENDER RISK AVERSION

Anthony struggled to find lenders "who will allow a second mortgage" on his deals, a common challenge in the current market. Toby explained that for real estate investment properties, a second lien significantly weakens the file as investors are more likely to walk away if things go south, making it tougher for the first lien holder to recoup their funds.

MODERN INVESTING DEMANDS NEW STRATEGIES

Toby emphasized that "everything's harder today than it was 5 years ago pre Covid and definitely harder than it was 10, 15 years ago." The days when HGTV made fix-and-flips seem "easy" are over, highlighting the need for sophisticated approaches and partnerships to navigate increased market complexities.

LEVERAGING DECADES OF REAL ESTATE MASTERY

Anthony, an "ex-athlete" and developer for "over 27 years," has hands-on experience across multiple real estate facets, including "fix and flips, through being a landlord, short term rentals." This deep, practical knowledge allows him to offer mentorship in partnerships, proving his value beyond just capital, even while recovering from a TBI.

STRATEGIC LENDING FOR RESIDENTIAL & MULTI-FAMILY

Toby confirmed his company focuses solely on "residential, family, and multi-family all day long," including "new construction," specifically excluding office, strip, or retail centers. This specialized lending approach allows them to "come in and partner" with experienced developers like Anthony who need additional capital for suitable deals.

CAPITAL PARTNERSHIP OVER COACHING FOR EXPERTS
